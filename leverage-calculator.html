Leverage Calculator

What is Leverage Calculator?

The Leverage Calculator helps you understand how leverage affects your position size and risk. Leverage allows you to control a larger position with a smaller amount of capital. While leverage can increase profits, it also increases potential losses. This makes it essential to calculate your exposure before entering a trade. For example, using 10x leverage means a small price movement can significantly impact your balance. This calculator helps you see those effects clearly. It is widely used in crypto and forex trading. By using this tool, you can manage your risk better and avoid overexposure.

How to use it?

  • Enter your margin (your own money)
  • Enter leverage (e.g. 10 for 10x)
  • Enter entry price
  • Enter exit price
  • Click calculate

Who uses this?

Traders who use leverage to amplify their gains or losses.
