Risk of Ruin Calculator

What is Risk of Ruin?

Risk of ruin measures the probability of losing your trading capital over a series of trades. It is one of the most important metrics for traders because it focuses on survival, not just profit.

Why It Matters

Even profitable strategies can fail if risk is too high. Managing your risk per trade and understanding your win rate helps you avoid blowing up your account.
