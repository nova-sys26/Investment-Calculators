What is Risk Reward Calculator?

The Risk Reward Calculator helps you compare the potential loss and potential gain of a trade before entering it. It shows whether a setup is worth taking based on how much you risk versus how much you can make.

How to use it?

  • Enter entry price
  • Enter stop loss
  • Enter take profit
  • Click calculate

Who uses this?

Traders who want to check whether a trade setup offers enough upside for the risk taken.
