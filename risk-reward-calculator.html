What is Risk Reward Calculator?

This tool compares how much you risk versus how much you can gain in a trade.

How to use it?

  • Enter entry price
  • Enter stop loss
  • Enter target price
  • Click calculate

Who uses this?

Traders who want to evaluate if a trade is worth taking.
