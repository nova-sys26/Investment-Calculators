Average Price Calculator


What is Average Price Calculator?

This tool calculates your average purchase price when you buy an asset at different price levels. It helps you understand your true entry cost.

How to use it?

  • Enter the price you bought the asset
  • Enter the amount you bought
  • Add multiple purchases if needed
  • Click calculate to see your average price

Who uses this?

Crypto and stock investors who buy the same asset multiple times and want to track their real average cost.
