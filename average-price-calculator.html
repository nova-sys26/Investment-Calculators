Average Price Calculator


What is Average Price Calculator?

An Average Price Calculator helps investors determine the true average cost of an asset when buying at different price levels. Instead of guessing or manually calculating, this tool gives you a clear view of your average entry price based on multiple purchases. When you invest over time, especially in volatile markets like stocks or cryptocurrencies, prices constantly change. You might buy an asset at $100, then again at $80, and later at $120. Without proper calculation, it becomes difficult to understand your real cost basis. This calculator solves that problem instantly. By entering the price and quantity of each purchase, the tool calculates your weighted average price. This allows you to see whether you are currently in profit or loss and helps you make better decisions about when to sell or buy more. This tool is especially useful for traders who use strategies like averaging down or scaling into positions. It removes confusion and ensures accuracy, which is critical for managing risk and maximizing returns. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced investor, using an average price calculator can significantly improve your financial decision-making.

How to use it?

  • Enter the price you bought the asset
  • Enter the amount you bought
  • Add multiple purchases if needed
  • Click calculate to see your average price

Who uses this?

Crypto and stock investors who buy the same asset multiple times and want to track their real average cost.
