What is Stop Loss Calculator?

A Stop Loss Calculator helps traders determine the exact price level at which they should exit a trade to limit potential losses. Risk management is one of the most important aspects of trading, and setting a proper stop loss is essential for long-term success. Instead of guessing where to place your stop loss, this tool calculates it based on your entry price and risk percentage. For example, if you enter a trade at $100 and are willing to risk 2%, your stop loss will be set at $98 for a long position. This ensures that you never lose more than your predefined risk. The calculator works for both long and short trades. In a long trade, the stop loss is placed below the entry price, while in a short trade, it is placed above. This tool automatically adjusts based on your input. Using a stop loss consistently helps protect your capital and prevents emotional decision-making. It is widely used by forex, stock, and crypto traders who want to trade with discipline and control.
