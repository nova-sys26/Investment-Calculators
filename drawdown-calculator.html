Drawdown Calculator

What is Drawdown?

Drawdown represents the percentage loss from your account peak. The deeper the loss, the harder it becomes to recover.

Why It Matters

Losses are not linear. A 50% loss requires a 100% gain to recover. This is why managing risk is critical in trading and investing.
