Liquidation Price Calculator

What is Liquidation Price?

The Liquidation Price Calculator helps you determine the price at which your leveraged position will be automatically closed. This is critical for risk management. When trading with leverage, your position can be liquidated if the market moves against you. Knowing your liquidation price helps you avoid unexpected losses. For example, a small price drop in a high-leverage trade can trigger liquidation. This tool calculates that level so you can plan accordingly. It is especially important for crypto futures traders. Use it to set safer positions and protect your capital.

How to use it?

  • Enter your entry price
  • Enter leverage
  • Click calculate
