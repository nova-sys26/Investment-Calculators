Loan Calculator

What is a Loan Calculator?

This calculator helps you estimate your monthly loan payment, total interest, and overall cost based on your loan amount, interest rate, and term.

How to use it?

  • Enter loan amount
  • Enter interest rate
  • Enter loan term
  • Click calculate

Who uses this?

Borrowers who want to understand loan costs and payments.
