What is BTC Dominance?

BTC dominance is Bitcoin’s share of the total cryptocurrency market cap. A rising dominance value often means money is rotating toward Bitcoin or away from altcoins. A falling dominance value often means the opposite.

How this page works

Sayfa doğrudan CoinGecko API çağrısı yapar. Cloudflare Worker, ekstra proxy ya da ayrı backend yoktur.

BTC Dominance = Bitcoin Market Cap ÷ Total Crypto Market Cap × 100

Why this is useful

  • Quick market structure read
  • Helps compare BTC strength vs altcoin strength
  • Useful for rotation, risk, and timing context
  • Better than raw price alone for sentiment framing
