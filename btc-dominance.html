What is BTC Dominance?

BTC dominance is Bitcoin’s share of the total cryptocurrency market cap. A rising dominance value often means money is rotating toward Bitcoin or away from altcoins. A falling dominance value often means the opposite.

How this page works

Sayfa doğrudan CoinGecko API çağrısı yapar. Ek proxy, Cloudflare Worker ya da ayrı backend yoktur.

BTC Dominance = Bitcoin Market Cap ÷ Total Crypto Market Cap × 100

Why this is useful

  • Quick market structure read
  • Helps compare BTC strength vs altcoin strength
  • Useful for rotation, risk, and timing context
  • Better than raw price alone for sentiment framing

Frequently Asked Questions

What does a higher BTC dominance mean?
A higher reading means Bitcoin is taking a larger share of the crypto market cap. That often happens when traders are more defensive.
Is BTC dominance a buy or sell signal?
No. It is a market context indicator, not a standalone trade signal.
Why use direct API instead of Cloudflare Worker?
It is simpler to deploy. The downside is that CORS or rate limit issues can still happen depending on the API and hosting setup.
