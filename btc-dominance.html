What is BTC Dominance?

BTC dominance is Bitcoin’s share of the total crypto market cap. When that share rises, Bitcoin is taking a larger portion of the market. When it falls, capital is usually spreading more into altcoins and other crypto assets.

The number matters because it gives a fast read on market structure. It does not predict price by itself, but it shows who is leading the market.

How this page works

The page fetches current market data from CoinGecko for the live stats cards. The chart itself is a real TradingView BTC.D chart, so you get proper historical market structure without using localStorage.

Why it matters

BTC dominance helps you judge whether the market is concentrating into Bitcoin or rotating toward broader crypto risk.

Interpretation:
Higher BTC dominance often points to a more defensive market structure. Lower BTC dominance often points to stronger altcoin participation.

Use cases

  • Altcoin rotation checks
  • Bitcoin-led vs. broad-market phase reading
  • Crypto allocation context
  • Market regime comparison
