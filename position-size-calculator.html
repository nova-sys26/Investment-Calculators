Position Size Calculator

What is Position Size Calculator?

The Position Size Calculator helps you determine how much to invest in a trade based on your risk tolerance. It is one of the most important tools for risk management. Instead of guessing how much to invest, this calculator uses your account size and risk percentage to give you an exact position size. For example, if you only want to risk 2% of your account on a trade, this tool calculates how much you should invest. This helps traders stay consistent and avoid large losses. Proper position sizing is key to long-term success in trading.

How to use it?

  • Enter your account balance
  • Enter risk percentage
  • Enter stop loss level
  • Click calculate

Who uses this?

Traders who want to manage risk and avoid losing too much on a single trade.
