What is Pip Calculator?

The Pip Calculator helps forex traders determine the value of a single pip movement in a trade. A pip is the smallest price change in most currency pairs, and understanding its value is essential for managing risk and position size. This tool allows you to calculate how much you gain or lose per pip based on your lot size and the current market price. For example, in a standard lot, one pip can be worth around $10 depending on the pair. Using this calculator helps traders make more informed decisions and better control their risk exposure. It is especially useful when planning trades or setting stop loss levels.

How to use it?

  • Enter your lot size
  • Enter pip size (usually 0.0001)
  • Enter current price
  • Click calculate

Who uses this?

Forex traders who want to calculate pip value before entering a trade.

Disclaimer: This is a simplified estimation. Actual pip value may vary depending on broker conditions and currency pair.
