What is a Crypto Profit Calculator?

A crypto profit calculator helps traders estimate the real outcome of a trade by comparing entry price, exit price, trade size, and fees. It is useful because small price moves can create a big difference in profit or loss, especially when trade sizes are large.

This calculator works for both long and short positions. That means you can use it to test bullish and bearish scenarios before deciding whether a trade is worth taking.

How the Calculator Works

For a long trade, profit comes from buying low and selling higher. For a short trade, profit comes from selling high and buying back lower. Fees are applied to both entry and exit so the result is closer to the real net outcome.

Long profit = (sell price − buy price) × amount − fees
Short profit = (buy price − sell price) × amount − fees
Return % = net profit ÷ entry value × 100

Why This Matters

Many traders focus only on price movement and ignore fees. That can make a trade look better than it really is. A calculator like this helps you see the true outcome before you commit capital.
