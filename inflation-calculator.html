Inflation Calculator

What is Inflation Calculator?

The Inflation Calculator shows how inflation reduces the purchasing power of money over time. It helps you understand what your money today will be worth in the future.

How to use it?

  • Enter your current amount
  • Enter expected inflation rate
  • Enter number of years
  • Click calculate

Who uses this?

Investors, economists, and anyone planning long-term finances.
