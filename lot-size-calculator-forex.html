Lot Size Calculator (Forex)

What is Lot Size Calculator?

A Lot Size Calculator helps forex traders determine the exact position size they should use based on their account balance, risk percentage, and stop loss distance. Proper position sizing is critical for managing risk and maintaining consistency in trading. Many traders make the mistake of choosing a random lot size, which can lead to excessive losses. This tool eliminates that risk by calculating the optimal lot size for each trade. For example, if you have a $1,000 account and risk 2% per trade, your maximum risk is $20. If your stop loss is 20 pips, the calculator determines the correct lot size so that a loss at that level equals $20. This ensures that your risk remains controlled regardless of market conditions. The calculator assumes standard forex conditions where 1 lot equals $10 per pip. While actual values may vary slightly depending on the currency pair, this provides a reliable estimation for most trades. This tool is essential for forex traders who want to protect their capital, avoid overtrading, and build a sustainable trading strategy.

Who uses this?

Traders who want to control risk and choose the right position size.
