What is a Profit / Loss Calculator?

A Profit / Loss Calculator helps traders and investors quickly measure how much they gained or lost on a trade. By entering your buy price, sell price, and quantity, you can instantly see the outcome instead of doing the math manually.

This matters because a trade is not just about price direction. The size of your position, your fees, and your exit price all affect the final result. A proper calculator helps you understand the real outcome of a trade before you scale up risk.

How This Calculator Works

The calculator first multiplies the buy price by the quantity to get your initial investment. Then it multiplies the sell price by the same quantity to get the final value before fees. If you enter fees, those are subtracted from the trade result to show the net profit or loss.

  • Buy Price = the price you entered the trade at
  • Sell Price = the price you exited the trade at
  • Quantity / Units = how many units you bought
  • Fees = commissions or trading costs

Why Profit / Loss Matters

Many traders focus only on direction and ignore the size of their position. That is a mistake. Even if your trade is correct, high fees or poor position sizing can reduce your real return.

This calculator helps you answer the question that matters most: Did this trade actually make money after costs?

⚠️ A trade that looks profitable on paper can be less profitable after fees.
